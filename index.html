OUR MISSION

Umoja basketball's mission is to grow the game of basketball in South Africa by creating a high school culture of recruiting, developing and preparing the young athletes for a potential career in Basketball.

WCSS X UMOJA PARTNERSHIP

MERLIN MOSOKO

Founder

Umoja basketball was foundered in 2014, by Merlin Mosoko in Cape Town, South Africa as a way to keep the youth of Delft of the streets and away from all the negative things. As time passed Umoja basketball became one of the Top Basketball Youth Development Programs in Cape Town with help from organizations such as Adonis Musati, Africa Unite and UNCR.

Meet coach Merlin! A father, mentor and local sports development fundi!
